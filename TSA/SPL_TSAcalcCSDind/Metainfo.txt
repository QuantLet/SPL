Name of Quantlet: TSAcalcCSDindicators

Published in: Statistical programming languages - Student Project on "Twitter Sentiment Analysis regarding Critical Slowing Down (CSD) indicators."

Description: For a given csv-dataset of Tweets, a time series of the share of positive resp. negative sentiment Tweets is generated. For this time series autocorrelation, variance, skewness, and W2 measure (see Drake, J., Griffen, B., 2010) are calculated in a moving (overlapping) window approach for multiple window sizes.

Keywords: Twitter, Tweets, Fast-Fourier-Transform, moving window, sliding window, autocorrelation, lag-1-autocorrelation, skewness, variance

Author: Justin Bugeja, Michael Kostmann, Mario Peplinski

See also: SPL_TSAtestFUNmw, SPL_TSAtestFUNrSD

Submitted: 12.08.2018

Datafile: tweets_raw.csv

Input: tweets_raw.csv

Output:  positve_acf.csv, positive_var.csv, positive_skew.csv, positive_W2.csv, positive_ts.csv, negative_acf.csv, negative_var.csv, negative_skew.csv, negative_W2.csv, negative_ts.csv
