Name of Quantlet: TSAplotCSDIndicators

Published in: Statistical programming languages - Student Project on "Twitter Sentiment Analysis regarding Critical Slowing Down (CSD) indicators."

Description: A function to plot the range of indicators (lag-1-autocorrelation, skewness, variance) that are measured through the SPL_TSAcalcCSDind Quantlet. The output graphs can me manipulated by changing the initial conditions in the script.

Keywords: Range, autocorrelation, lag-1-autocorrelation, skewness, variance

Author: Justin Bugeja, Michael Kostmann, Mario Peplinski

Submitted: 12.08.2018

Input: ../SPL_TSAcalcCSDind/positive_var.csv, ../SPL_TSAcalcCSDind/negative_var.csv, ../SPL_TSAcalcCSDind/positive_acf.csv, ../SPL_TSAcalcCSDind/negative_acf.csv, ../SPL_TSAcalcCSDind/positive_skew.csv, ../SPL_TSAcalcCSDind/negative_skew.csv
